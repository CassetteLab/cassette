Cassette 1.8.4

NEW
• Redesigned playlists — immersive full-screen artwork, color-matched theming, and a smoother editor. Edit a playlist right in place: swap the cover (photo or generated gradient), reorder and remove tracks, and add music without leaving the page.
• Redesigned artist pages — immersive header, latest release, most-played tracks, large album artwork, and related artists.
• Deleting a playlist now lets you choose: remove the playlist only, or also delete its downloaded files.

FIXED
• Lock Screen / Control Center: skip and scrubber controls no longer show up greyed out.
• End of an album/queue no longer triggers stray "phantom" playback — it now stops cleanly, and resumes from the top if you press play.
• Volume slider no longer desyncs or jumps to random positions on launch.
• Deleting a playlist from its detail view now updates the library right away.
• Removing downloads now reliably frees up disk space.
• Fixed a crash that could happen while scrubbing.

• Lock your device while playing, then try skip and scrubbing from the Lock Screen.
• Edit a playlist's cover and tracks; delete a playlist both with and without downloaded files.
