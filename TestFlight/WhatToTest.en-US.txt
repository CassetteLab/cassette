New in 1.8.2

• A new inline queue in the full-screen player — swipe between the player and Up Next, with an animated transition.
• Drag to reorder the queue (iOS and macOS).
• Drag to reorder pinned items on Home (and in the macOS sidebar).
• A "New Playlist" quick action on Home.
• The full player now zooms open from the mini player and runs edge-to-edge.
• Confirmation toasts when you pin, add to queue, or play next.

Improved

• Full-player artwork fills the space — bigger cover, no empty gap below the title.
• Tapping the cover no longer opens lyrics (use the lyrics button); the grabber now closes the player.
• Sharper queue typography and contrast, with a refreshed active-track pill.
• Full-resolution artwork in Wrapped and the macOS full player.

Please focus on: queue reordering on both platforms; opening and closing the full player (zoom in, swipe or tap to dismiss); pinned reordering persisting across relaunch; and the queue ↔ player transition.
